  • On LtG, your profile is called your "hand" because we are each giving our hand to make the world a more beautiful place. HAND POWER!!

    CONNECT 4 CHANGE - Introduce yourself and invite people to contact you about volunteer opportunities and to brainstorm change! Since everything shared on LtG has the intention of linking a gap, all communication is meaningful, thoughtful and creative. Together, we are grasping the communication potential of the internet!

    GAPS - How can we grow if we just don't know? (more info coming soon)

    UPDATES - What each other are currently doing to LINKtheGAP. Find short and sweet textual updates revolving around projects and experiences individuals are currently working on.

    PLEDGES - Pledges are personal commitments from being inspired, tangible ways that you decide to contribute to making our world a more beautiful place. It feels nice to have a record of everything you have committed to doing.

    INSPO - Share inspirational sayings that make each other want to reach out for change! These can be from something you read or better yet come straight from your dome! Give credit to original author if you feel it necessary.

    SKILLS - Each and every one of us bring gift... each of our gifts are unique and limitless. Share any life skills (can be something you just wish to improve) and for each skill give yourself a rating out of 5 stars. Match and link!

    Your hand is an opportunity to connect for change! Also, it as a personal record for you to reflect back upon at any time for strength and motivation.
