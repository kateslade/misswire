LINKtheGAP is a non-profit organization and social media platform that is specifically designed for anyone to make social, humanitarian and environmental change.

There will be 3 main sections to make change on LINKtheGAP’s website…

GAPS: User-generated digital library where anyone can post first-hand stories and research to learn from the diversity of our experiences about global issues, big or small.

INITIATIVES: World map where anyone can directly contact grassroots Initiatives to set up volunteering at home or abroad and can see updates straight from each Initiative about the change we are helping to make.

MARKETPLACE: Hand-made Marketplace to shop directly from creators who are using fair-trade, eco-friendly and cruelty-free practises while also giving back a percentage of each sale to support an Initiative of the customer’s choice.

LINKtheGAP’s promise is to be 100% fee-free, ad-free and volunteer-run forever!
