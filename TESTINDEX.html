100% free and volunteer-run

Absolutely ALL use of LINKtheGAP is 100% fee-free and always will be, because of dedicated volunteers who are reliable, self-motivated and have a desire to make global change. Our initiative is created and powered entirely through volunteerism, by people like you, who are sharing their unique skills to collaborate. Together, we are building a social networking platform to collectively empower individuals to create change at the ground level.

"Then how is LINKtheGAP's platform financially sustainable?"

LINKtheGAP is a grassroots Initiatve just like the Initiatives that we support. We raise money transparently on a goal by goal basis to cover our maintenance costs, what other non-profits refer to as "overhead." LINKtheGAP is deeply rooted in accountability and transparency and is completely different and transforming how we make change.

It's all worth it when we get the feels from helping the world!

There are 3 main sections to make change on LINKtheGAP’s website…

Gaps

User-generated digital library where anyone can post first-hand stories and research to learn from the diversity of our experiences about global issues, big or small.

Initiatives

World map where anyone can directly contact any Initiative to set up volunteering at home or abroad and can see updates straight from each Initiative about the change we are helping to make.

Marketplace

Hand-made Marketplace to shop directly from creators who are using fair-trade, eco-friendly and cruelty-free practises while also giving back a percentage of each sale to support an Initiative of the customer’s choice.

Together, we are volunteering our time to co-create the most effective make-change network ever!
